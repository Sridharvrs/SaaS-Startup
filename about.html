We're building the analytics layer for the next 10,000 companies.

Nebula started in 2021 when three data engineers got tired of stitching together five tools just to answer one question. We believed analytics should feel instant, collaborative, and built into the product — not bolted on as an afterthought.

What we believe

Principles, not posters.

01

Speed is a feature

If a dashboard takes longer to load than to think about, it's broken. We obsess over milliseconds because your time is the bottleneck.

02

Data is a product

Metrics deserve the same craft as UI. Versioned, tested, documented, and shipped — not dumped in a wiki no one reads.

03

Default to open

Internal transparency builds trust. We default to sharing what we build, how we decide, and what we got wrong.

04

Hire for range

We'd rather hire one person who can span three disciplines than three who each do one. Curiosity beats credentials.

The journey

From a hackathon to 12,000 teams.

Mar 2021

Idea at a hackathon

Three engineers build a real-time query engine in 48 hours. Wins "Most Likely to Be a Company." It is.

Aug 2021

First paying customer

Orbit signs on for $200/mo. We deliver from a shared apartment. The dashboard still runs in production today.

Jan 2022

Seed: $5.2M

Led by Founders Fund. We hire our first six engineers and ship the AI co-pilot prototype by summer.

Jun 2023

Series A: $14M

1,000 customers. SOC 2 Type II. We open the SF office and ship 220 integrations by year-end.

Feb 2025

Series B: $42M

12,000 teams. 4.2B events/day. We launch embedded analytics and our enterprise tier. The rest is still ahead.
