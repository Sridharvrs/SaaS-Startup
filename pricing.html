Billing questions

Frequently asked.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes prorate automatically and reflect on your next invoice. No contracts lock you in on the Starter or Growth tiers.

What counts as an "event"?

Any row ingested into Stackly — a page view, a transaction, a backend log line. Deduplicated and sampled transparently so you only pay for what you actually query.

Is there a free trial of paid plans?

Every workspace gets a 14-day trial of Growth features on signup. No credit card required. You keep your data and dashboards if you stay on Starter afterward.

Do you offer discounts for startups?

Yes — eligible early-stage companies get 50% off Growth for the first year. Reach out via the contact page with your deck or website.
